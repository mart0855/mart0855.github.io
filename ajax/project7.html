Motion Graphics

Sportsnet

While at Rogers Media I had the pleasure of putting together some motion graphics and edit some video clips for Sportsnet's social media platforms.

Brevity was key in creating these assets as the attention span for social media is under 30 seconds. The infographic took on a guessing game approach as facts are given about a certain hockey arena, but the answer is never given, thus driving the viewer to the Sportsnet website to find out more.

The Toronto Blue Jays clips were assembled from a shoot that gathered all the players on the team into a "Jiggedy Box" lighting studio. These short teasers were created to build hype for the upcoming baseball season by showing hints of the players followed by quick cuts that show a dynamic representation of their position and persona.
