Infront Consulting Website

Infront Consulting needed a website refresh to go along with their new branding, modernizing their image as a competent IT consulting firm.

Working with the team at Octopus Ink I was tasked with creating a detailed design mockup for the Infront website that suited their new re-brand while including parallax capabilities.

I was able to provide a consistent look and feel by using colour blocking techniques to separate content while following a unifying grid and colour pattern throughout.

Visit the live Infront Consulting website to see it in action.
