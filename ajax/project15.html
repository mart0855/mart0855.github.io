BSTREET Agency Reel

A showcase for a marketing agency presenting their multi-channel campaigns using techniques in video editing and motion graphics.

BSTREET invited me to help tell their story through an Agency Reel which demonstrates the range of solutions they offer. Their ideas and creativity are revealed through sequential case studies explaining how they integrate shopper marketing, brand experience and digital activation to achieve results for their clients.

I was happy to help them summarize this work in a way that highlights their successes in an introductory teaser video along with individual case studies for in-depth analysis.
