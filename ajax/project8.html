Acrylic Labrador

Painting is one of my many side projects that has become a frequent request from friends and family.

Pet portraits are a popular subject and by immortalizing them in a painting secures the animal's place on their owners wall the same way it has transfixed a place in their hearts. Capturing the character of the animal can be just as difficult as that of a human being, especially during the photography sessions.
