Brand Identity
Shepherds of Good Hope
Shepherds of Good Hope is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving the poor and homeless in Ottawa. This inital concept study created while interning at McMillan sought to refresh their identity as a place that fosters inclusion. The symbol of the weave is meant to convey stability and support to those who need it. By sustaining basic human needs, Shepherds of Good Hope helps their clients self-actualize by becoming part of a community.
