Caring for Elders

Pencil drawings take a lot of time and focus. The medium lends well to the subject matter of ancestry and time that are themes in the Caring for Elders illustration.

This was an exploration in visual dynamics to tell a story about how we must cherish the time we have with our aging family members, as those moments can make a difference in our lives.

The image was created to accompany an article about preventing elder abuse in institutions by volunteering time and adding another pair of eyes.
