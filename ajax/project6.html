FX Fearless
Broadcast Package
A motion graphics template for the popular television network, FX, this piece demonstrates how a brand can be relayed into the gamut of new media. Focusing on the shape of the wordmark itself, polygonal shapes create a framing apparatus on which kinetic typography can be displayed to introduce upcoming programs or transition between commercial breaks. The rigid assemblage of elements is consequently liquefied after a short time to resemble how fear of the unknown can take the form of unyielding confrontation or frantic flight.
