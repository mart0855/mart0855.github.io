Modern Space Discoveries

This infographic was created for a magazine spread featuring Canada's presence on the timeline of international space discoveries.

The main challenge was to depict a multitude of breakthroughs in space exploration within a seamless chronological timeline. By using a textured iris effect on the main illustration, there is a feeling of peering into the past through a video camera.

The grid in the background reflects a digital mapping system that is plotting some key points in the timeline overlaid onto the scene. Historical accuracy was a motivation that was used to great effect as an educational tool while creating unity among all of the elements.
