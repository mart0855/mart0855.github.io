Web marketing

Texture

Joining the digital creative team at Rogers Media, I took on a role as the digital designer for one of their licensed companies, Texture™ Canada, a magazine subscription app with unlimited reading.

I was tasked with designing all of the bilingual digital marketing assets for the Q4 campaigns of 2016. This included web banners, social media ads, email templates, landing pages and motion graphics. With so many offers, I made it my mandate to provide consistency across all of the campaigns while highlighting some of the great features Texture™ has to offer. Developing responsive emails using new technologies such as Movable Ink brought dynamic content to eblasts, giving them a competitive edge.
