#Protect Your Net

Condo Compass is a web application designed to help manage the business affairs of condominium corporations via their board of governors.

Using design-first principles, a small team of researchers devised this object-oriented interface to record minutes documents and track actions of the board. All of the essential functions for managing a business are collected in the left column of tabs that reflect real world objects. This affordance along with the simple and aesthetically beautiful UI makes meetings less of a chore, while educating those with less business experience on how proper meeting agendas are executed.
