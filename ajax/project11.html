Graphic Design

HandPick Logo

HandPick is a recruitment platform that seeks to humanize the job seeking process by connecting talent directly with employers.

The logotype is organic with a vibrant green hue to represent a fresh new way of hiring or applying for jobs. To create this logo, I went through a process of writing using thick mediums and making the "k" into a finger and thumb.

After delivering the logo to the client, I decided to re-interpret the design using a typeface that could work well as a monogram at smaller sizes. By revisiting work, I discover new ways of enhancing my process and becoming more efficient as a designer.
