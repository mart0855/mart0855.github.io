About

My name is Alex Martin and I believe that...

Imagination is a defining feature that we as humans possess, allowing us to transcend our own reality and discover that we have no boundaries. Art is one way to harness this freedom of thought and present it in a tangible form of communication. Design unfurls the abstraction of art into a transparent plane of understanding. For me, design is a means of widespread dissemination that provides windows into a global network of ideas that I want to be a part of.
