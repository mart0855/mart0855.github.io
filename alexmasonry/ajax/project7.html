Hand-made Dreams

A quote book about dreams created using hand-made typography and a single font-family.

Each spread is uniquely representative of its content, physically emoting the subject matter of the excerpt. The wooden manuscript casing and rolled up scroll within attest to the history of bookbinding, reviving the reverence for the print medium.
