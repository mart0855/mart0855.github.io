Graphic Design
The Laramie Project

Winning poster design chosen to represent the Algonqin college theatre production of The Laramie Project, a play by Moisés Kaufman and members of the Tectonic Theater Project. Based on a true story, the play is a re-interpretation of interview accounts of the people of Laramie, Wyoming which document their reactions after the murder of Matthew Shepard; a hate crime against a gay man.

This poster seeks to present the violence in a way that captures the viewer’s attention without totally revealing the details. The copy is set in American Typewriter to further emphasize the factual and serious content of the play. By attracting an audience to the play, the poster took a small role in delivering the message of tolerance.
